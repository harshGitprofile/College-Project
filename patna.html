1.Patna

Mahavir Mandir

Patna, Bihar


Description


Mahavir Mandir, located near Patna Junction, is one of the most revered Hanuman temples in India, attracting devotees from all over the country. The temple is known for its belief in the miraculous powers of Lord Hanuman, especially on Tuesdays and Saturdays, when thousands gather to offer prayers and seek blessings for strength and protection. The temple complex is modern, yet the rituals and traditions practiced here are ancient and deeply spiritual. The “Naivedyam” prasad of the temple is famous among devotees. Visiting this temple is considered auspicious and is said to fulfill the wishes of the faithful.
  • Location: Patna, near Patna Junction
  • Specialty: Renowned Hanuman temple known for its miraculous powers.
  • How to Reach:Located near Patna Junction, easily accessible by road and rail.
  • Best Time to Visit:Tuesdays and Saturdays, highly auspicious for Lord Hanuman’s worship.
